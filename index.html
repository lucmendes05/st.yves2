Hidden in my skin is their faith,
With my blemishes emerge a mosaic,
My scars each a piece of this puzzle; memorising the past I have lived,
The red spilling from my veins like port,
Paint on a canvas every shade of peach I dry with the softest of blues

The clouds bleach the purple sea,
Dotted with shy guardians painting the tapestry,
Breaths mirrored in the silver of lonely bedrooms - the souls of hundreds weaved this web,
Embraced the tight curls with the mountains
